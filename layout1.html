Colombian Food

Colombian food is way different in the morning colombians can be eating calentado witch is chicharone whith pork with eggs and rice with beans and plantains for breakfast sometimes if they wanted to it can also bring sausage. In the afternoon colombian will always eat a snack like snack a chicken empanada or if they wanted something sweet they also get a cholado is like sweet milk with fruits and this juice drink underneath they also add whipcream and a cherry.Also in like very hot days and in cold days or when you are sick colombians also eat sancocho is a soup made with vegetables pork and yuca.
